Background



In 1988, Franklin was born in South Los Santos. His mother, a cocaine addict, died when he was very young, and his father departed before he was even born. Franklin then resided in South Los Santos with his grandparents. He met JB Bradshaw and Tonya Wiggins here. He began his career as a street hustler and frequently peddled cigarettes alongside Bradshaw until his grandfather caught him one day. Franklin and his aunt Denise then moved into a home on Forum Drive after his grandparents passed away. In his grandmother's testament, the home was passed down to them.
