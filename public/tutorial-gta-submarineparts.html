Submarine Parts

In GTA V, there are submarine parts that can be collected. Abigail Mathers has a Strangers and Freaks task that involves gathering 30 submarine parts. This does not count towards 100% completion like spaceship parts, and letter scraps




1. Completes the mission The Merryweather Heist, Freighter or Offshore
2. Michael have to buy a property Sonar Docks at Paleto Cove and then speak to Abigail.
