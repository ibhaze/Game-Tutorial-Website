Flying UFO (100% Required)

After achieving 100% completion in the game, you can spot a UFO hovering above Fort Zancudo. This particular UFO is positioned at approximately 80% height, so if you don't spot it right away, try lowering your altitude and continue searching. With persistence, the UFO should eventually come into view.

Above Mount Chiliad, you can find a UFO. On its deck, a message is scribbled, saying "Come back when your story is complete." If you revisit this location after achieving 100% completion in the game, a UFO will appear, but only when the weather is cloudy and the in-game time is 3:00am. Once you meet these specific conditions, you'll be able to witness the ultimate UFO sighting.

After reaching 100% completion, you can fly a helicopter to the hippie camp, which features various alien murals and symbols. If you ascend to the highest altitude possible in the helicopter, you'll eventually come across the Sandy Shores UFO. When you parachute just below this UFO, you might observe a circular door opening and a radiant beam of light shining down. However, it's worth noting that you won't be pulled up into the ship despite the intriguing sight.

Underwater UFO (100% Not Required)

The Sunken UFO Wreckage in Grand Theft Auto V is an Easter egg, alongside four other UFOs, that can be discovered within the game. Unlike the rest, this particular UFO does not require players to achieve 100% completion of the game and is non-operational.

The Sunken UFO Wreckage can be found north of Procopio Beach, off the coast of San Andreas. It looks like the UFO crashed into the ocean and sank to the bottom. It's mostly intact but has some damage from the crash. There are weeds and debris on top, suggesting it's been there for a while.
