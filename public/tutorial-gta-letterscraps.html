Letter Scraps

The Mystery of Leonora Johnson in Grand Theft Auto V involves collecting Letter Scraps, which are special items scattered throughout San Andreas. There are a total of 50 Letter Scraps that can be collected right from the start of the game. These scraps appear as glowing pieces of paper on the ground and emit a faint sound of paper rustling when you're nearby. Once you pick up a Letter Scrap, it is automatically saved, so you don't need to collect it again.

Upon collecting all 50 Letter Scraps, a 4-page letter is revealed, exposing the identity of the killer and triggering a new mission called "A Starlet in Vinewood." This unlocks further progression in the storyline related to Leonora Johnson's mystery.
