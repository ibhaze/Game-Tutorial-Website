How to control AK-47 Recoil

As you can see from the video, the AK-47 doesn't shoot at one point straight all the time. To shoot properly, you need to control where it shoots. In the video, we can observe that the shooting goes up, then to the left, then to the right, and then again to the left, repeating the cycle. So, in order to control the recoil, you need to practice, just like in the video. To practice, go to any map that has straight walls and input the following commands: "mp_roundtime_defuse 60" (this will make the round last for 60 minutes), "mp_buy_anywhere 1" (this will allow you to buy anywhere on the map), "mp_buytime 6000" (so that you have an unlimited amount of time), "sv_cheats 1" (to be able to use any cheats you want to), "endround" (to end the small round), and "mp_afterroundmoney 16000" (to have the full amount of money to be able to buy weapons). After you have entered these commands, you can start your training. First of all, try to understand how the recoil moves when you shoot without controlling it. Once you've done that, try to practice just like in the video: shoot and move the crosshair to the bottom and left. After you've tried to control these two movements to be able to shoot in one exact point right from the beginning, move on to controlling it when it goes to the right and left again. When you've done this, congratulations! You've learned how to control recoil for the AK-47. Practice it even more, and then you will be able to control it just like a professional CS gamer. Please note that cheating and using external commands to modify the game experience is not supported in official gameplay and can be against the terms of service of the game. It is recommended to practice within the confines of the game's rules and mechanics.
