Events of GTA 5



In the quest Far Out, Omega can be seen in the Grand Senora Desert. When Franklin approaches him, he orders Franklin to stop where he is, calls him a carbon-based living form, and points an odd object at him. Franklin is given an explanation of how a spaceship crashed and its components are all around San Andreas. Franklin asks him if he's crazy before leaving. He requests Franklin to locate the parts once more as he is leaving.
