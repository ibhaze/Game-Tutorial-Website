Background



Michael was raised in a poor household and was born somewhere in the late 1960s. His drunken, violent father abandoned him when he was a little boy. His mother told him numerous tales about his father's reasons for leaving, never knowing for sure whether he had run away, been struck by a train, or enlisted in the Navy. His mother soon followed suit, leaving him by himself. In high school, he excelled in athletics and was featured in the local media, but injuries and a violent temper made him realize that sports weren't for him.
